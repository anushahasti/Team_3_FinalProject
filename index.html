COVID-19 Information

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is a highly infectious disease that causes most infected people to experience mild to moderate respiratory illness, but those with underlying health conditions can develop serious illness.

The virus spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose and is why safety protocols are important, such as social distancing and wearing a mask.
